Help

Display Control Bar

  • Airports can be selected by clicking "APT SELECT".
  • When an airport is selected, its available maps can be seen next to the "MAPS" button in the Main Menu.
  • Functionality of greyed out buttons in the Display Control Bar will be implemented at a later date.

Keybinds

  • E - Toggle data source between ATC 24 and Event server.
  • P - Toggle Predicted Track Lines. Wait up to 3 seconds to see change.
